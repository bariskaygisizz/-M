## Description
Davetly helps you design elegant digital invitations for weddings, engagements, henna nights, circumcision celebrations, ballet recitals, graduations, birthdays and more.

FREE features:
• Create up to 3 invitations
• 5 free edits per invitation
• Basic templates
• Share with Davetly watermark

PLUS / PRO subscriptions unlock unlimited edits, premium templates, PDF export, RSVP links and remove branding.

Subscriptions auto-renew unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. Manage in your Apple ID / Google Play account settings. Restore Purchases is available in the app.
